How it works

From job post to
shortlist in hours.

Three steps. No scheduling chaos. No bias. Just ranked candidates ready for your review.

STEP 01
📋

Build your AI Interview

Define the role, set your must-have criteria, and choose interview format — voice, video, or text. Shortlis8 generates tailored questions aligned to your job spec in under 2 minutes.

STEP 02
🤖

AI interviews every candidate

Share a link. Candidates complete their AI interview anytime, on any device. Our interviewer asks adaptive follow-ups, probes deeper, and evaluates responses in real-time — 24/7.

Receive a ranked shortlist with scores, summaries, and recommendation tags the moment interviews complete. Your team reviews only the top candidates — human time, well spent.
